# Kinetic Gain Embedded Tie-back

This repo demonstrates the **sensor drift monitoring** primitive — robot telemetry posture, calibration evidence, blind-zone pressure, and operator-safe corrective action visibility.

For Kinetic Gain Embedded, that matters because robotics and industrial autonomy products often need:

- telemetry-safe fleet summaries
- evidence-ready corrective action history
- operator-safe read surfaces for review boards
- low-risk analytics hooks inside the product without turning the app into a raw observability firehose

Embedded extends this into in-product fleet reporting, autonomy confidence signals, and operational review layers that remain safe, observable, and revenue-relevant.
